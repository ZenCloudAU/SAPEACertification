## What the Exam Actually Tests

Based on community experience from certified practitioners:

- **Not** just TOGAF knowledge — SAP EAF is different from generic TOGAF
- **Not** just memorisation — scenario-based reasoning throughout
- **SAP-specific terminology** — Strategic Priority, Value Driver, Metro Map variants
- **SAP Reference Architecture** — RBA and RSA content and structure
- **Real customer scenarios** — choosing the right architecture for a business need
- **Diagram comprehension** — Metro Map, four-view matrix, EA Alignment Model

### The Three Questions to Ask in Every Scenario

1. What is the business problem?
2. What solution fits best?
3. Why are the other options wrong?

## Study Approach (Community Recommended)

1. Read the Preparation Guide first
2. Work through Learning Journeys in order — **do not skip diagrams**
3. Attend case study live sessions — do it twice
4. Apply concepts to real customer scenarios as you learn
5. Use SAP Business Accelerator Hub to explore industry-specific architectures
6. Review score reports if you need a second attempt — target weak domains

> *"Focus on understanding over speed. Consistency, deep understanding, and applying what you learn in real-world scenarios are the keys to success."*
